Missing pages that would generate leads directly

!

No "Hire Me" landing page — You have a /contact but no conversion-focused "Hire Me" page explaining your availability, process, and what clients can expect. This is the #1 lead gen page for freelancers.

Create /hire-me with: availability status, how to work with you, what info to bring, response time, and a streamlined contact form.

!

No case studies section — "Show, don't tell" is the top trust signal for developers. Competitors with case studies close 3x more leads than those without.

Create /case-studies with 3–5 detailed project breakdowns: problem, solution, tech stack used, outcome metrics, client quote.

~

Pricing page needs a lead capture — /pricing exists but likely has no CTA to get a custom quote. People who visit pricing pages are high-intent buyers.

Add "Get a Custom Quote" form on /pricing. Ask: project type, budget range, timeline, contact info. This qualifies leads before they email you.

~

No free resource / lead magnet — Informational visitors don't convert immediately. Give them a reason to leave their email.

Create a free resource: "Web App Development Cost Calculator" (interactive tool), or "Laravel vs Node.js: Which is Right for Your Startup?" PDF guide. Collect emails via a simple form.

CTA improvements

~

Single CTA = "Hire Me" everywhere — Not every visitor is ready to hire. You need three CTA tiers: (1) "Book a Free Call" for warm leads, (2) "View My Work" for discovery phase, (3) "Get a Quote" for ready-to-hire.

~

No urgency or availability signal — Add "Currently accepting 2 new clients for Q2 2026" near your CTAs. Scarcity increases conversion rates significantly.

~

No WhatsApp button — For India/UAE market, WhatsApp is the #1 business communication channel. Add a floating WhatsApp button with pre-filled message: "Hi Arun, I'd like to discuss a project."
