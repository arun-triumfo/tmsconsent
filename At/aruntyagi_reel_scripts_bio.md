## REEL 2 — "Hard work se success nahi milti"
**Format:** Bold text on screen + voiceover | **Length:** 25–30 sec | **Type:** Controversial Opinion

### Script:

**[HOOK — 0 to 3 sec]**
"Mehnat karo, mehnat karo, mehnat karo —
ye advice bakwaas hai. Sunoge?"

**[BODY — 3 to 22 sec]**
"Main 3 saal tak sirf 'hard work' karta raha.
Result? Thakaan. Frustration. Zero progress.

Kyunki hard work sirf tab kaam karta hai jab direction sahi ho.

Galat raaste par bhaago — aur thak jao.
Sahi raaste par chalo — aur result aata hai.

Mehnat important hai. Lekin pehle poochho:
'Main kahan ja raha hoon — aur kyun?'

Bina direction ke hard work = busy rehna = successful dikhna = actually kahin na pahunchna."

**[CTA — 22 to 30 sec]**
"Agree karte ho? Disagree karte ho?
Dono case mein — comment karo.
Controversy se darr nahi lagta mujhe."

**Format:** B-roll morning routine + voiceover | **Length:** 45–55 sec | **Type:** Relatable + Value

"Alarm baja... snooze maara... 3 baar.
Ye thi meri roz ki kahani."

**[BODY — 3 to 40 sec]**
"Main raat ko sochta tha — 'kal pakka 5 baje uthunga.'

'Tu raat ko so nahi raha — tu kal se bhaag raha hai.'

No scrolling. No reels. Kuch nahi.

Alarm se pehle.

Koi app nahi. Koi hack nahi.
Bas apne brain ko signal dena — 'raat ko rest hai.'

**[CTA — 40 to 55 sec]**
"Try karo aaj raat — phone 10 baje band.
Kal mujhe batana result.
Aur ye reel save karo — kal subah yaad aayega."
